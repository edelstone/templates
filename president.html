President Denise M. Trauth

Welcome to Texas State!

I invite you to take a few minutes and learn more about the people and history that make Texas State University an institution rich in diversity, academic programs, and pleasant surroundings.

Our distinguished faculty and dedicated staff make Texas State University one of the top public university choices in Texas. We’re proud of what we offer; our faculty take the responsibility of nurturing individuals and cultivating character seriously. We value the modeling and teaching of honesty, integrity, diligence, courage, compassion, fairness, and respect. Our extensive list of distinguished alumni, such as President Lyndon B. Johnson, stands as testimony to the University’s proud legacy. And the list continues to grow with each passing year.

Stay for a while and browse. I am proud of Texas State University’s resources and world-class academic community, and I’m certain you will be, too. The more you learn, the more you’ll understand why Texas State feels like home.

- Denise M. Trauth
